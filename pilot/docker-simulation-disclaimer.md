# Docker Simulation Disclaimer

## Scope of Simulation

This Docker-based environment is a **conceptual simulation**
intended solely for research and demonstration purposes.

It does not represent any real telecommunications network.

---

## Data Characteristics

All data generated within this environment is:

- Synthetic
- Randomized
- Non-operational
- Non-identifying
- Non-persistent

No real traffic, signaling, or subscriber data is used.

---

## Technical Boundaries

This simulation:

- Does not connect to external networks
- Does not intercept or inspect traffic
- Does not emulate real operator configurations
- Does not implement lawful interception or monitoring

---

## Legal and Regulatory Position

This simulation:

- Does not require regulatory approval
- Does not fall under telecom operational regulations
- Does not process personal data as defined by GDPR

---

## Intended Audience

This environment is intended for:
- Concept validation
- Architectural discussion
- Standards exploration

It is **not intended** for production, deployment, or enforcement.
